Survey

Hello! My name is Dmytro, I'm 25 years old. I come from a small village in Vinnytsia region.

I have an incomplete higher education (bachelor's degree) - studied at the National Aviation University, specializing in computer engineering, with a focus on computer systems and networks.

I haven't worked in the field of my education :)

In the company where I work, I am involved in both internal and external logistics in one of the departments.

I don't have any commercial IT experience. When I was studying at the university during summer vacations, I came across an unconventional guide on HTML and CSS, and for me, it was like discovering a fantastic blockbuster in the wonderful world of the WWW. After reading it, I became interested in this field and started watching video tutorials on YouTube about website development and practicing a bit. I liked that with minimal time spent, you could see some results in the browser, and that was captivating. When I gained some knowledge (or so I thought back then :) ) in web development and perhaps a bit of audacity, I decided to venture into job searching. I succeeded, but it was a learning experience. I sent my resume to several web studios, and I got a response from the CODERIVER web studio. I completed their test assignment (more or less) and they invited me to their training, which potentially could lead to a permanent position. Since it was winter and the holidays were approaching, I assessed my real chances of getting an offer from this studio, and after the third day of training, I went home for the holidays :)

So, for certain reasons, I stopped pursuing further learning in front-end development. Now that I have more free time, I've decided to return to learning. I learned about Beetroot from a Google search. I was choosing among several schools, and Beetroot appealed to me the most. I liked the price of the course. The cost of the course was important to me - it just needed to not be too cheap. I liked the curriculum, the instructor, and also the reviews on DOU.

Decision made, so here we go!
